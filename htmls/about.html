About GoalCraft

Our Mission

GoalCraft is on a mission to create the ultimate productivity and time management suite. Our innovative tool combines the best productivity techniques and tools into one powerful application. Imagine a tool that helps you manage your time, prioritize tasks, set goals, stay focused, and seamlessly integrate with your existing calendars.

We believe in enhancing productivity and making time management a breeze. Our team is dedicated to developing an application that not only meets your requirements but also offers an intuitive and engaging user experience.

Pomodoro Technique

Manage your time effectively with our Pomodoro timer, designed to boost your productivity and keep you focused.

Task Management

Organize and prioritize your tasks with ease using our comprehensive task management system.

Urgent Important Matrix

Identify and prioritize your tasks effectively with our Urgent Important Matrix, gamified for a fun experience.

Link Organizer

Keep all your important links in one place and open them in bulk with our link organizer feature.

Goal Setting

Set, track, and achieve your goals with our goal-setting feature that helps you break down goals into actionable tasks.

Focus Timer

Stay focused with our focus timer that includes meditation and focus music to help you concentrate.

Calendar Integration

Seamlessly integrate with your Google Calendar to keep track of your schedule and never miss an important event.
